About Us

Guopeng Global Healthcare Management (Chengdu) Co., Ltd.

Guopeng Global Healthcare Management (Chengdu) Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive enterprise specializing in healthcare supply chain management and medical services. For over a decade, with its headquarters located in the Chengdu High-Tech Free Trade Zone-a national-level pilot free trade area-the company has continued to deepen its expertise in the medical industry. From its origins as a regional enterprise rooted in Southwest China, it has grown into an industry leader with extensive supply chain resources and a mature service network both domestically and internationally.

Leveraging profound industry experience, the company has established long-term strategic partnerships with more than 300 high-quality brand manufacturers worldwide. Its product portfolio covers a wide spectrum of fields, including medical imaging, radiotherapy equipment, life science instruments, medical robots, smart wearable devices, surgical instruments, endoscopy systems, medical diagnostic equipment, critical care devices, disposable consumables, and customized hospital products. Furthermore, the company offers integrated solutions such as planning, design, and construction for digital smart hospitals, operating rooms, ICUs, central oxygen supply systems, and laboratories. To date, it has served more than 200 secondary and tertiary medical institutions, with business operations extending across over 30 countries and regions. Adhering to the corporate philosophy of "Caring for Lives, Serving Health, " the company relies on high-tech products, professional expert and technical service teams, and advanced operational models to build collaborative platforms that promote health and mutual benefits for governments, medical institutions, enterprises, and public organizations.

Driven by the mission to "Innovate and Reshape Integrated Medical Equipment Solutions, " we leverage strong supply chain integration and service innovation capabilities to actively respond to the multi-level health needs of doctors, medical institutions, and families. We are committed to bringing professional medical-grade products and services into more scenarios, enhancing public health quality, and advancing the healthcare industry toward a smarter, more accessible, and warmer future.
